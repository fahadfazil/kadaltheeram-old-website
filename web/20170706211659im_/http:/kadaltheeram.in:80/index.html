Kadal is sea and theeram is shore – Kadaltheeram is a beach, it is also the name of our real special resort, from where you enjoy the pristine climate, serene backwaters and the soothing power of Ayurveda.

Varkala beach has several specialties. The Ancient Janardhana Temple, the mineral water springs, cliff beaches and the sight of sea meeting backwaters make it a special treat for everyone.
Take a short ride in a country boat and feel the mesmerizing effect, which makes you want to enjoy the ride even more, while trying your hand at fishing, seeing kids jumping into the water and picking coconuts that come floating along.Maybe you will also like to join locals collecting shrimps, picking crabs and trying a plate of kappa (tapioca) with fish curry.
